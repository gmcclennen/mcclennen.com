This site is being created as a place to remember Robert James “Bob” McClennen and Karen McClennen, and to share information about their memorial gathering.

Memorial Gathering

A memorial celebration for Bob and Karen will be held on Saturday, August 8, 2026.

Location and time will be posted here soon.

For friends and former students who cannot attend in person, a Zoom option is planned. Details will be posted here before the service.

About Bob

Robert James “Bob” McClennen devoted 31 years to teaching science at Springfield High School, where he inspired generations of students and was known affectionately as Mr. Bear.

After retirement, Bob and Karen made Cape May their home. Bob volunteered extensively in the Cape May birding community, including work with Purple Martins and monarch butterflies.

About Karen

Karen McClennen shared Bob’s love of Cape May, community, and service. More about Karen’s life and legacy will be added here.
